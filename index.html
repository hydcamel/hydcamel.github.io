Research Interest

 I am interested in the security issues in Cyber-Physical systems (CPS) and general networked systems.I also have broad interest on optimization and machine learning. I would like to understand the problems theoretically and tackle them with performance guaranteed algorithms. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Network state recovery after joint cyber-physical attack
  • Defending coordinated cyber-physical attack through secured sensor placement
  • Detection and localization of attack through graph neural network
