Research Interest

 My research interests lie in applying optimization theory and machine learning tools to improve networked systems. I would like to understand the problems theoretically, tackle them with performance guaranteed algorithms, and validate them through system implementation. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Inference and Control in Overlay Networks. We hope to infer the latent sharing among the seemingly independent overlay tunnels without the help from underlay networks.
  • Prevention and Recovery of Cascading Failure in Smart Grid
  • Detection and localization of attack through graph neural network

In the past, I have spent 4+ years on applying machine learning for wireless communications.
